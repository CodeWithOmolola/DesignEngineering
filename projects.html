Ore Mi Stickers

Product Design & Development

Like graffiti that claims space on city walls, our labels are declarations that evolve beyond simple definitions. The "Ore Mi" series, meaning "my friends" in Yoruba, explores how the identities we claim are territories we stake in a world constantly trying to categorize us. Your label might say "Designer" or "Artist," but it's the journey underneath, the grind, the growth, the doubts, that gives meaning to the name you take on. Just as graffiti exists as a powerful statement of presence, our identities shift and adapt while we find our own way to leave our mark.
