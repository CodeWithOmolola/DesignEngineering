ABOUT

As an interdisciplinary designer and visual storyteller, I transform creative practices into narrative tools of self-discovery and problem-solving. Operating through Lab Studio, I explore the intersections of art, culture, design, and innovation—bridging disciplines to reimagine how creative thinking can address complex challenges.
My work emerges from a belief that design is a language of possibility. By acquiring skills across multiple domains, I craft experiences and solutions that challenge conventional boundaries. Whether developing brand identities, conceptualizing products, or mapping experimental design strategies, I approach each project as an opportunity to build new worlds and challenge existing standards.
Through Indstndrd—short for 'Industry Standard'—my design platform, I aim to make these innovative approaches tangible and accessible, turning conceptual thinking into actionable design experiences.
